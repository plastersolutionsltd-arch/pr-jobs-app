WARRANTY & GUARANTEE

25-Year Manufacturer Product Warranty All silicone render systems installed by P&R Solutions — EcoRend, K Rend, and Baumit — carry a 25-year manufacturer product warranty against material failure, provided the system is installed strictly to the manufacturer's published specification. Documentary evidence available on request.
5-Year Workmanship Guarantee P&R Solutions provides a 5-year workmanship guarantee covering all defects directly attributable to our installation method — including system delamination, substrate debonding, and render failure arising from our workmanship. Internal plastering works carry a 12-month workmanship guarantee.
What Is Covered Defects in the installed system arising from our application: delamination from a sound substrate, significant cracking in thin-coat render systems attributable to installation, and colour inconsistency arising from product application.
What Is Not Covered Damage caused by structural movement or subsidence; dampness penetrating from an independent source; physical impact or vandalism; modifications by a third party; pre-existing substrate conditions not identified at survey; failure to maintain per manufacturer guidance; or normal weathering and surface soiling.
Guarantee Conditions: All guarantees run from the date of practical completion. Any repair or modification carried out by a third party without written consent from P&R Solutions will render the workmanship guarantee void. To make a claim, contact us in writing at Plastersolutionsltd@gmail.com — we will attend site within 10 working days of a valid claim.
